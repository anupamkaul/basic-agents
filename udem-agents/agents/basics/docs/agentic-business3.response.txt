### Why Personalized Healthcare Management?

- **Chronic Disease Burden:** Chronic diseases like diabetes, cardiovascular conditions, and mental health disorders constitute a majority of healthcare costs and patient morbidity.
- **Data Explosion:** Wearables, electronic health records (EHRs), genomics, and patient-generated health data create an enormous data pool ripe for AI-powered insights.
- **Treatment Complexity:** Personalized treatment plans require integrating vast data across time, patient preferences, and clinical guidelines, which is difficult for humans alone but ideal for an agentic AI.
- **Patient Engagement:** Continuous monitoring and nudges can improve adherence and outcomes, reducing hospitalizations.
